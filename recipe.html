Best Test Recipe

This is my Grandma's recipe. It is the best and I make it all the time

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Oranges
  • Garlic (lots of garlic)
  • salt and pepper
  • Oregano

Recipe

Skin and slice apples and oranges. Add some chopped garlic and oregano. Salt and pepper to taste. Bake at 350 for 2 hours

Serves: 400

Time: 2 hours
